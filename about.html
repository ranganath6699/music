ABOUT US

THE ROOKIES, an electrifying Desi hip-hop band, are setting the music scene ablaze with their innovative sound. This dynamic trio, consisting of Joe, Vish, and Skandy, blends traditional Desi melodies with the pulsating beats of hip-hop, creating a genre-defying musical experience. Joe's charismatic vocals, Vish's masterful production skills, and Skandy's lyrical prowess combine seamlessly, crafting tracks that resonate with audiences worldwide. Their music is a cultural journey, reflecting their roots while pushing boundaries, making The Rookies a rising force in the music industry. With catchy lyrics, infectious rhythms, and a vibrant stage presence, The Rookies promise an unforgettable musical ride for fans of all genres.
