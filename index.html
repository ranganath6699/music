About the Crew

The Rookies, a dynamic force in the world of Desi hip-hop, bring a fusion of passion and vibes that ignite the spirit of their listeners. Hailing from the heart of India, their music tells the tales of the streets, the dreams of the youth, and the heartbeat of a nation. With every verse, The Rookies carve a sonic landscape that takes you on a journey through the bustling streets, the vibrant colors, and the soulful rhythms of India. Their lyrics are sharp, painting vivid pictures of the trials and triumphs of Desi life. The beats are like a heartbeat, pulsating with the energy of a generation determined to make their mark. The Rookies are not just a band; they're a movement, a testament to the power of music and the unyielding spirit of Desi hip-hop. Get ready to groove, feel the passion, and immerse yourself in the vibey world of The Rookies, where the beats are infectious, and the lyrics are on fire.
