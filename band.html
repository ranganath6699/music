THE ROOKIES

We love music!!

The electrifying Desi hip-hop sensation, The Rookies, are setting the music scene on fire with their innovative sound. This dynamic trio, consisting of Joe, Vish, and Skandy, weaves traditional Desi melodies into the pulsating heart of hip-hop, creating a genre-defying musical experience that ignites the spirit. Joe's charismatic vocals, Vish's masterful production skills, and Skandy's lyrical prowess form a musical trinity that transcends boundaries, crafting tracks that resonate with audiences worldwide. Their music is a mesmerizing cultural journey, a tapestry that reflects their roots while pushing the boundaries of musical exploration. The Rookies' infectious rhythms are like sonic spells that ensnare your senses, beckoning you to dance to the beat of their cultural fusion. Their vibrant stage presence transforms concerts into electric spectacles, uniting fans of all genres under the banner of their extraordinary sound. As The Rookies continue their upward trajectory, they promise an unforgettable musical adventure for enthusiasts seeking more than just melodies and lyrics; they offer an immersive experience that transcends the ordinary and catapults them into the pantheon of music's rising legends. Get ready to embark on a sonic journey with The Rookies, where catchy lyrics and pulsating rhythms become the soundtrack to a vibrant cultural revolution.
